‚úÖ Eligibility & Opt-In Requirements

To keep prize distribution fair and manageable, brewers must actively opt in to be eligible for prize packages.

  • Opt-In Required: Brewers must complete the official CHBC prize opt-in form to be eligible for any All-State or Division prizes.
  • Local Pickup Confirmation (Division Prizes): When opting in, brewers in the North and South Divisions must confirm that they are able to pick up prizes at the designated MoreBeer! showroom.
  • Deadline: Opt-in must be completed by the published deadline (to be announced on the main dashboard and Discord). Brewers who do not opt in by the deadline will not be eligible for prize packages.

üîÅ Prize Roll-Down Policy

If a brewer in a prize-eligible position is not eligible or unable to receive a prize, that prize will be awarded to the next eligible brewer:

  • If a top finisher did not complete the opt-in form, their prize spot is forfeited and all lower prizes shift down.
  • For Division (North/South) prizes, if a brewer cannot reasonably pick up at the MoreBeer! showroom, their prize position is forfeited and all prizes shift down to the next eligible brewer.
  • Prize roll-down continues until all available prize positions are either filled by eligible brewers or exhausted.

Example: If the 1st place South Division brewer does not opt in, the 2nd place brewer moves up to the 1st-place prize package, 3rd place moves up to the 2nd-place package, and the 4th-place brewer (if eligible) receives the 3rd-place package.
