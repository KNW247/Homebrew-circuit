‚úÖ Eligibility & Opt-In Requirements

All-State & Division Prizes

  • Any brewer who appears in the final All-State or Division standings (based on competitions included in the CHBC schedule) and completes the 2026 Prize Eligibility Opt-In Form is eligible to be considered for prizing.
  • All-State prizes may be shipped anywhere in the United States that MoreBeer! is able to fulfill orders to. Division prizes are local pickup only and are intended to support local California brewers who can claim prizes in person at participating MoreBeer! showrooms.

2026 Prize Eligibility Opt-In Form

  • To be eligible for any 2026 CHBC prize package (All-State or North/South division), brewers must complete the 2026 Prize Eligibility Opt-In Form.
  • The opt-in form will confirm:
    • Which division(s) you are competing in (North and/or South).
    • Your shipping details and/or your ability to pick up prizes in person at a qualifying showroom.

Prize Cascade / Shift-Down Rule

If a brewer in a prize-winning position is not eligible for a prize (for example, they did not complete the opt-in form or are unable to pick up locally), that prize will shift down to the next eligible brewer in the standings for that category.

Example: If the 1st place brewer cannot claim a division prize, the 1st place package moves to the next eligible brewer, and all lower prizes cascade accordingly. Prizes will not be held, re-shipped, or re-assigned outside of these terms.
