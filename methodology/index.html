The California Homebrew Circuit (CHBC) is a free, community-driven initiative connecting homebrewers across the state through friendly competition, shared learning, and cross-club collaboration.

How Competitions Are Selected

To be included in the CHBC standings, competitions must meet the following criteria:

  • BJCP-Sanctioned: Only competitions sanctioned by the Beer Judge Certification Program (BJCP) are considered.
  • California-Based: The competition must be held within the state of California.
  • Open and Inclusive: Events must be accessible to the broader homebrewing community.
  • Standard Format: Specialty competitions limited to specific styles, ingredients, or themes are not eligible.
  • Results Transparency: Competitions must publicly share complete results using a standardized format.

Each season, we aim to include 10â€“12 well-established and geographically diverse competitions. Selection favors events with a history of strong participation, quality judging, and reliable results reporting.

Result Standardization

To ensure fairness and consistency across the circuit, all competitions must publish results that:

  • Clearly identify brewers, beer names, BJCP categories/subcategories, and award placements
  • Be available via public link, official website, or verifiable documentation
  • Be posted in a timely manner following the event

CHBC reserves the right to delay or exclude competitions from the current season if results are incomplete, unverifiable, or posted in nonstandard formats.
